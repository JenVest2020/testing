How I fell in love with Digital Technology

Art that soothes the soul

2004:

A broken heart is a searching heart.
After the dissolution of my first marriage, a friend, knowing I was becoming depressed, gifted to me a digital art software cd-rom that she no longer used. She encouraged me to just play around with it and claimed this new hobby was a great therapeutic tool as well. I can no longer remember the name of the software. Only that it did, indeed, become a very effective therapeutic tool for me. I would spend hours exploring all of it’s special effect plug-ins and create small design pieces I thought were quite beautiful and pleasing for me to look at. I’ve always loved art but lacked the talent and skill to make my hands create the designs in my mind. To me, this software was revolutionary and I was hooked.
